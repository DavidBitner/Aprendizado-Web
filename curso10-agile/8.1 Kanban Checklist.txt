1. Determine if physical or digital Kanban board is right for your team
2. Create the Kanban Board (physical, Jira, Trello, Kanbanize, etc.)
3. Visualize your work (create work units or user stories)
4. Create a team Working Agreemtn - definition of ready and Definition of done
5. Come up with explicit polivies that work out for the team
5. Define and assign Work in Progress limits to Kanban columns
6. Identify and discuss impediments or blockers that will prohibit the team's momentum forward
7. Determine a cadence (repetition) of optional team meetings (sprint retrospective, prioritization session, backlog grooming, etc.)
